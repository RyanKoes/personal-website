Chemical Engineering Car Club

Chem-E car is a student club that builds a shoe-box size car that runs and stops solely on chemical reactions. In the Fall and Spring semesters of my senior year, I was the Electromechanical team co-president. Our team is responsible for managing the hardware and software components of the car. This includes designing the mechanical parts and programming the control systems.

Our car competes in the regional AIChE competition, and if we win, the team advances to the national competition. The team competed in nationals in the Fall semester of 2024.

Nifty Fund Project Lead

I was approved for funding and lead a team of engineers to develop an automatic pour-over coffee maker. The system is designed to follow the V60 pour-over brewing method. It also will allow for precise control over water temperature, water flow, brewing time, and brew strength to provide cafe-level quality coffee.

Our current prototype is featured in the picture. The final design will be presented at the end of the spring semester, 2026.
